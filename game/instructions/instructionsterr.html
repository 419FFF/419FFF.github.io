instructions:
Saving and loading:
-DO NOT press the green flag while your world is open, it will delete the world if you do that.
-Paste the text from the save menu into a text editor - such as notepad - and save it to your computer to save the world.
-Copy this text back into the "load game" menu to load a world.
~~~ Gameplay Control Defaults ~~~
A / D - Walk
W / Space - Jump
Move Mouse - Target item
Click - Use item
0 to 9 - Select inventory item
Mouse Scroll - Zoom in / out
E - Open Inventory
E (Over tile / NPC) - Interact, such as open chest
P - Pause Menu
M - Open Map
C - Toggle Smart Cursor
S - Go down platform
H - Check housing
Left / Right arrow - Navigate inventory HUD
~~~ Map Controls ~~~
WASD - Move
Mouse Scroll - Zoom in / out
M - Close Map
~~~ Inventory Controls ~~~
E - Close Inventory
Mouse Scroll - Navigate crafting menu
W / S keys - Navigate crafting menu
Mouse click and hold - Pickup and move item
~~~ Other Controls ~~~
L - Toggle FPS
T - Enter Command
